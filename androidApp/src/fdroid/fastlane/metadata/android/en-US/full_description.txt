Bici Radar helps you check public bike availability across supported Spanish cities.

This F-Droid flavor keeps the core bike-station experience while removing proprietary mobile SDKs.

What you can do:
- browse supported cities and stations
- save favorite stations
- use commute shortcuts and widgets
- launch external routing with free-software compatible geo intents
- receive monitoring notifications

This flavor intentionally avoids Firebase, Google Play Services, Garmin Connect IQ, and in-app review/update SDKs.
The embedded Android map is degraded in this build to a station-focused list and location summary.
