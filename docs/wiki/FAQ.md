# FAQ

## Is BiciRadar real-time?

BiciRadar uses official city open-data feeds and updates frequently, but timings depend on each provider's refresh rate and availability.

## Why do numbers sometimes not match what I see at the station?

Station occupancy can change in seconds, and some providers publish data with slight delays.

## Does the app need location access?

Yes, for nearest-station and distance-based features. Without location permission, results can be less accurate.

## Can I use voice without opening the app?

Some actions can answer directly in Siri/Shortcuts (and on watch), while others open the app for navigation or richer context.

## Does BiciRadar support all cities?

BiciRadar supports many Spanish cities and networks. Availability can change depending on each provider feed.

## Where do I report issues?

Use the support/contact channel linked from the app or website.
