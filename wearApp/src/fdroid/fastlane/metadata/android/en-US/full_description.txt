Bici Radar Wear brings public bike station availability to Wear OS.

This F-Droid flavor is published as its own package and removes proprietary crash reporting and wearable sync SDKs.

What you can do:
- check your favorite station
- browse nearby station availability
- use Wear OS tiles
- add complication data to your watch face

The F-Droid flavor is designed to stay fully buildable without Google Play Services wearable runtime dependencies.
